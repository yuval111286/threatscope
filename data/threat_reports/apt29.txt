Threat Group: APT29 (also known as Cozy Bear)
Description: APT29 is a Russian cyber espionage group associated with the Russian Foreign Intelligence Service (SVR).
They are known for long-term stealthy intrusions into government and research networks.

Recent Activity:
- 2023: Phishing campaigns using compromised Microsoft 365 accounts.
- 2024: Leveraged the PowerShell backdoor "GraphicalNeutrino" to exfiltrate data.
- Common Tools: Cobalt Strike, PowerShell Empire, Mimikatz.
- Techniques: Spear-phishing, credential dumping (MITRE ATT&CK T1003), lateral movement via RDP (T1021).

Mitigation:
Use multi-factor authentication, monitor PowerShell execution, and block suspicious outbound connections.
